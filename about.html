About Me

My Story

Hello, my name is Yudan. Nice meeting you here!

Currently, I am a UX/UI designer specializing in front-end development. Prior to this, I used to be a product lead, a co-founder, a senior R&D supervisor, and a Ph.D. in Educational Psychology, but I’m very grateful that I ended up doing what I’ve always been passionate about, design, and all my past experience led me where I am today.

I grew up loving to create which comes in a range of drawing, designing, crafting, and knitting. However, I was discouraged from pursuing a career in related fields which is not uncommon in Asian families, but I have never given up on drawing and designing, and kept learning by doing.

In the meanwhile, I developed an interest and passion for psychology and received my degrees in Educational Psychology from the University of York in England. I have ever since worked on a wide range of projects that involved understanding how people think, feel, and behave in order to create and provide services that are intuitive, effective and engaging. That’s when I started realizing that being a UX designer is what I have always wanted to do because it is a perfect combination of what I’m good at [understanding and helping people] and what I am passionate about [design]. Now I am confident to say that I am very skilled in user research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, and usability testing, and have a strong track record of delivering high-quality designs that meet the needs of both users and business stakeholders. I just love to create fun, engaging, and functional experiences that empower my users.

In my free time, I enjoy staying up-to-date on the latest design trends and techniques. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a designer. Just in case I sound like a robot, I also enjoy traveling, drawing, working out, outdoor activities, movies, stand-up comedy, baseball and other millions of whirlwind interests that come and go, that’s how I become a multipotentialite, nothing is wrong with that! Right?! I certainly hope so=P
