Project 1: FITNICE

Your Journey to Fitness Starts Here…

Finding exercise routines that are right for you and also fit into your busy schedule can be extremely difficult. This is where this new fitness app, FITNICE, comes in. FITNICE is designed to provide the user with different ways to start small and gradually fit an exercise routine into their busy schedule.

My Process

Empathize

My first step was to spend considerable time researching my target users. In order to create a quality application that puts users' needs first, I needed to empathize with the users and understanding who they are, their goals, motivations, and "why" they want what they want.

I did my user research with 109 participants altogether:

  • Survey with 101 participants

I conducted a survey with 101 participants at the beginning of my research on fitness app to better understand my users and also customize my follow-up interview questions in order to validate my survey findings while gathering more and deeper additional information.

  • Interview with 8 Participants

I interviewed eight people who aged between early 30s and early 40s, and from different professional backgrounds. All the interviews were conducted virtually and completed in 30 minutes.

I found the most mentioned pain points are:

  • A sedentary job that doesn't allow a lot of time for exercising.
  • Busy schedule
  • Don’t know how and where to start as a beginner to working out.
  • Lack of motivation

Based on my research findings, I created my persona and user stories:

  • As a new user, I want to learn about different exercises, so that I can figure out what is best for me.
  • As a new user, I want to be shown how the exercises are done, so that I know I'm doing them correctly.
  • As a frequent user, I want to be able to schedule exercises for working out, so that I build positive habits.
  • As a frequent user, I want to be able to earn achievements or rewards, so that I can stay motivated.
  • As a frequent user, I want to complete daily challenges, so that I can have an additional way to stay motivated.
  • As a frequent user, I want to track progression and record what I've done, so that I can see my progress over time.
  • As a frequent user, I want to be able to share routines with my friends who may also be interested, so that I can encourage them to become healthier.

Define

  • The Objective

Motivate people into an exercise routine that suits their level, schedule, and interests.

  • The Challenge

The fitness industry is booming. With the rise in obesity rates, it’s becoming more important to provide people with a way to stay healthy and reduce their risk of developing lifestyle-related diseases. However, finding the motivation to get into an exercise routine is very difficult. It is also difficult to find an exercise routine that suits your level, fits in your busy schedule, and fits in with your interests.

  • The Product

FITNICE is a responsive application that is designed to help you develop a healthier lifestyle by providing interactive guides that include exercises, routines, and tips. You can take your pick from easy and short as 5 minutes routines to more difficult routines that take longer. It's all about starting slowly and steadily building up the amount of time and intensity of your exercise routines.

Ideate

This stage started with identifying the key features and functions of my design. This helped me focus on the most important aspects of the prototype and ensure that I would be testing the right things. At this stage, I created my user flow and a rough draft of my prototype by use of Balsamiq.

User Flow

Main Features:

  • Search and filter exercise videos (based on type, difficulty level, length, etc.) Exercise scheduler
  • Option to add sessions to calendar
  • Create user accounts
  • Tracking and charting of users’ progression over time
  • A game layer with individual daily challenges, achievements, and/or rewards
  • Social sharing for routines or favorite exercises
